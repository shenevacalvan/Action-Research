# Quick Start Guide

This guide provides an overview of how to get started with your Action Research project. Follow the steps outlined below to ensure a successful project completion.

## Steps to Follow:
1. Understand your research question.
2. Identify your data collection methods.
3. Develop a timeline for your project.
4. Communicate with stakeholders.
5. Document your findings systematically.
